Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for developers and tech enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. Over the years, it has grown into a global event, attracting thousands of attendees from all corners of the tech industry. From showcasing breakthrough innovations to fostering collaboration between startups and tech giants, TechCon has remained at the forefront of technology advancement.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire innovation, promote knowledge sharing, and drive the future of technology forward. We believe in empowering the next generation of developers, engineers, entrepreneurs, and tech leaders through accessible, engaging, and inclusive events.
